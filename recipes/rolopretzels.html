Rolo Pretzel Recipe

Description

Chewy caramel, crunchy pretzels, and colorful M&M's, rolo pretzels are the perfect salty sweet dessert! With only three ingredients, this recipe is simple and fast, making it the perfect choice for your next get together. Read through the steps below, then check out our other great recipes here!

Ingredients

  • Pretzels
  • Rolo's
  • M&M's

Steps

  1. Assemble Lay the pretzel squares out evenly in a single line on your baking sheet. Make sure each has a little bit of space in between. Then add one Rolo candy on top of each pretzel square.
  2. Bake Place the baking sheet in the oven and bake at 250°F for about 3 minutes, or just long enough for the Rolo’s to get soft.
  3. Top Remove the pan from the oven and then press a M&M on top of each softened Rolo. Make sure to let them cool completely before enjoying!
