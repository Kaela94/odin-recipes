Marshmallow Snowman

Spread some holiday cheer with these adorable marshmallow snowman treats! Easy to put together these simple desserts are perfect for any get togethers you may be having, and pair incredibly well with hot chocolate. Try it out for yourself then check out our other great recipes!

Ingredients

  • 18 marshmallows
  • 54 mini M&M candies
  • 6 pieces of Twizzlers pull ‘n’ peel cherry candy
  • 6 mini Reese’s peanut butter cups
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • ½ cup orange candy melts
  • ½ cup white frosting
  • 6 lollipop sticks

Steps

  1. In a small microwavable bowl, microwave chocolate chips for 30 seconds. Stir well then microwave in additional 15-second increments, stirring between each, until melted and smooth. Set aside. Repeat to melt orange candy melts; set aside.
  2. Slide 3 marshmallows onto each lollipop stick.
  3. Using a butter knife, place a small dab of frosting on the back of three M&M candies and place down the front of each snowman to resemble buttons.
  4. Using a toothpick and the melted chocolate, place dots on the snowman to form the eyes and mouth.
  5. Using a separate toothpick, dip it into the melted orange candy and use it to draw a nose on each snowman.
  6. Use a butter knife to add a small blob of frosting to the top of a mini Reese's peanut butter cup. Press the peanut butter cup onto the top of the snowman pop to form a chocolate hat.
